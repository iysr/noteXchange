About noteXchange

noteXchange is a free to use, note sharing platform! Share your notes, and get access to notes from others!

noteXchange was built with HTML, CSS and Vanilla JS. Vanilla JS was tidied up with Webpack. Cloud and Database functions are provided by Firebase. noteXchange is hosted by Netlify.
